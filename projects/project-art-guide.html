Project Summary

In a small team of 3, we created a new identity and website for the very outdated Art Guide brand. We created both a responsive logo, and a responsive website.

Problem Space

How might we provide information about Art events to the residents and visitors to the Pacific Northwest?

Process

We started our process with user research, which included both a survey and interviews. We then created paper prototypes and an Axure prototype, which we used in usability studies to ensure success.

After many iterations with our prototype we implemented our design using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
